About Me

Hello! My name is Tania Olivas and I am a current student at Eleven Fifty Academy enrolled in the Web Development course. I hope to one day become a Front End Developer and join the tech world.

After spending time as a Nursing Home Administrator for 2 years, I felt that my strengths did not quite line up with my role and decided to take a leap of faith and join the tech world.
