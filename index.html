About Me

Hello! My name is Tania Olivas and I am a recent graduate of Eleven Fifty Academy's Web Development course. As a first-generation college student and daughter of 2 immigrant parents, I know and embody the value of dedication and hard work.

I have previous experience as a Nursing Home Administrator, but then realized that some of my core values and strengths were not aligning well with my career and decided to change my trek. So, I decided to take a leap of faith and join the software developers of this world.

Although I am very new to the tech industry, I have already learned so much and am eager to learn much more. I pride myself in being self-motivated, detail-oriented, and enjoy problem-solving, all which align very well within the tech world. I hope to put some of my strengths to work and join a team as a Web Developer.
