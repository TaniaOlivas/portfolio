About Me

Hello! My name is Tania Olivas and I am a senior at Purdue University in Indianapolis studying Computer and Information Technology with a concentration in Web and Software Development. As a first-generation college student and daughter of 2 immigrant parents, I know and embody the value of dedication and hard work. With my passion for coding and learning, diverse background, strong communication skills and ability to collaborate well in a team environment, I am believe I can be an asset to the Software Developers of the world.

I have previous experience as a Nursing Home Administrator, but then realized that some of my core values and strengths were not aligning well with my career and decided to change my path. Continuous learning and growth are very important to me in a career and the tech industry vastly incorporates both of those. So, I decided to take a leap of faith, return to college and join the software developers of this world.

Although I am new to the tech industry, I have already learned so much and am eager to learn much more. I pride myself in being self-motivated, detail-oriented, and enjoy problem-solving, all which align very well within the tech world. I hope to put some of my strengths to work and join a team as a Software Developer.
